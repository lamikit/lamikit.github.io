**Qian Chen** is currently a Ph.D. student at **The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen)**, under the supervision of **Prof. Yaojing Zhang**. 

Previously, he served as a Laser Designer at the **China Academy of Engineering Physics (CAEP)**. He received his M.S. degree from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University** in 2024, advised by Prof. Jiangang Lu, and his B.E. degree from **Beijing Institute of Technology** in 2021.

### Research Interests
His current research focuses on **silicon-based optoelectronic devices** and their applications in:
* **Information Encryption**
* **Advanced Imaging**
* **Artificial Intelligence (AI) Algorithms**
