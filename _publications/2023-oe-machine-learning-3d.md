This work proposes a machine learning phase modulation scheme based on CNN and RNN for 2D/3D switchable displays.
* **Authors:** **Qian Chen (1st Author)**, Yumeng Zhang, and Jiangang Lu (Corresponding Author).
* **Journal:** *Optics Express*, Vol. 31, No. 12, pp. 19675-19688 (2023).
* **Impact:** Demonstrates higher optical efficiency and lower crosstalk in 3D displays using hybrid neural networks.
