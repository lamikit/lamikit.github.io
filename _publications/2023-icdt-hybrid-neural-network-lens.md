This research proposes a phase distribution optimization scheme based on a hybrid neural network to improve the optical properties of lenticular 3D displays.
* **Authors:** **Qian Chen (1st Author)**, Yumeng Zhang, and Jiangang Lu (Corresponding Author).
* **Venue:** *ICDT 2023 (International Conference on Display Technology)*, Volume 54, Issue S1.
* **Key Innovation:** Utilized optical simulation software (Dimos.2D and Lighttools) to build a dataset of 400 samples for training a neural network that realizes complex phase distribution of LC lenses, achieving lower crosstalk and higher peak illuminance.
