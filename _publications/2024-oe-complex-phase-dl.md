A deep learning-based phase modulation method for liquid crystal (LC) devices is demonstrated, particularly addressing the challenges in single-electrode and multi-electrode structures.
* **Authors:** **Qian Chen (1st Author)**, Weiping Ding, Feng Jiang, and Jiangang Lu (Corresponding Author).
* **Journal:** *Optics Express*, Vol. 32, No. 15, pp. 25883-25895 (2024).
* **Key Contribution:** Established a deep learning model to map phase retardation distribution onto electric field distribution, effectively mitigating fringe field effects and enabling precise phase modulation.
