This conference paper demonstrates a polarization-independent liquid crystal (LC) device designed for large optical phase depth, achieving 8.7π phase retardation at 532nm.
* **Authors:** Yumeng Zhang, **Qian Chen**, and Jiangang Lu (Corresponding Author).
* **Venue:** *ICDT 2022 (International Conference on Display Technology)*, Volume 53, Issue S1.
* **Key Innovation:** By using an LC layer with a twist angle of 180°, the device shows excellent polarization independence (lowered to 0.1%), showing great potential for optical communication and imaging systems.
