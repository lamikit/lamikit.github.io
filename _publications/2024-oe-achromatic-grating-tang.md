This research demonstrates a broadband wavelength designable achromatic grating integrated into a single-layer templated cholesteric liquid crystal (CLC) device.
* **Authors:** Mingyuan Tang, Changli Sun, **Qian Chen**, Weiping Ding, Jianxin Yang, Yumeng Zhang, and Jiangang Lu (Corresponding Author).
* **Journal:** *Optics Express*, Vol. 32, No. 12, pp. 20449-20462 (2024).
* **Key Innovation:** Different grating structures are integrated into a single-layer device, allowing the achromatic diffraction wavelength to be freely designed from the visible to the infrared range.
